# Traversal Modes — Timeline & Goto

Covers: timeline traversal (walk forward/backward chronologically from a neuron,
showing neurons in temporal order), goto traversal (follow edges to connected
neurons regardless of creation time, showing the edge reason for each connection).
Both are navigation commands, not search — no scoring, no ranking, just walking
the graph or timeline.

Requirements traced: §4.4 Traversal Modes.
Dependencies: #6 Neurons (timeline ordering), #7 Edges (goto follows edges).
